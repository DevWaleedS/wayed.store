About US

Wayed is an online shopping application that puts the wholesale market in your hands through an application that you can download on your mobile phone with ease.

Wade connects small business owners with distributors and suppliers directly within the online wholesale market.

If you are the owner of a grocery store, restaurant, coffee shop, or any other commercial establishment, you can now get all your needs from the best sources at wholesale price. Not only that, by shopping on the Wide platform, you can benefit from a distinguished set of exclusive endless offers and discounts on all products within the application.

In order to provide maximum convenience to all users, you can choose the most suitable for you from among several payment methods, in addition to express delivery service to any place in the Sultanate of Oman.

No more wasting time searching for products, no more negotiating the right price with merchants and distributors, no delays in shipping, now the Wide application provides you with all products at the best prices, while ensuring that they reach you safely and quickly.
